Rule engine — the four promotion gates
1 · WFYP achievement ≥ 75% of the annual grade target   2 · NOP achievement ≥ 50%
3 · Overall WAS above 100%  (75% × WFYP + 25% × NOP, each capped at 150%)   4 · Persistency ≥ 87% (new joiners exempt)
Computed on a rolling trailing-12-month window ending at the assessment month. Gate rules are fixed in the engine; the grade targets below update via the Designation Targets upload.
